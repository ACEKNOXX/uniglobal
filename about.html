About us

At Unitech Global Resources We plan, build, support and manage IT Infrastructure solutions. Helping organizations to assess design and efficiently operate their network, to unlock the full value of their investment and benefit from greater operational efficiency

At Unitech Global solutions are tailored to meet your specific requirements; Transmission and Fibre Optics network designs, installation,RF Planning and Optimization, Network Business Consultation, Site Acquisition, Project Management, Civil designs and Site build design and Implementation of Enterprise wide communication networks.
